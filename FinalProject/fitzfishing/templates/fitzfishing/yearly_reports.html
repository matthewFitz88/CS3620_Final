Yearly Reports

General Information

The Yearly Reports is all our data the we have gathered since 2018 broken down into years and then into each lake and pond specifically. Each year that is availible to you will give you the following information:

  • Slideshow showing some of our best catches from that year
  • Total fish caught for every month, season, and locations fished for that year
  • Percentages of all fish caught for month, season, and location
  • Rating breakdown
  • Graphs showing all of the previously meantioned
  • Links to all the locations fished during that year

Ratings

Each year is given a rating breakdown and all locations fished during each year are given a rating as well. The ratings are broken into five different levels. Those being: slow, fair, average, good, and hot. This rating is based on the average fish caught pertrip during each year.

You will see that on each years page that the rating levels are given a number range. The way we get these numbers is by using the average fish caught over all trips from that year. The average rating will be set by the years average and then the fair and good ratings are the next four numbers above or below the average and then the slow and hot are any numbers above or below those. Below is an example of how this works using an average of 10.

  • Slow: 1-5
  • Fair: 6-9
  • Average: 10
  • Good: 11-14
  • Hot: 15+

Lake Data and Statistics

At the bottom of every years page there will be lakes and ponds that you will be able to select from to see it's data and the stratigies that we used to catch fish there from that year. The first thing that will be on these pages is a table of when, how many, and what species of fish we caught for each trip during that year. There will also be the total, number of trips, average, and rating at the bottom of the tables. Below that there will be a table showing how many of each species we caught along with the percentages.

Below the tables will be the stratigies that we used to catch those fish at that location during that year. One thing to keep in mind is that the amount of information for each location will very depending on how many times we went and how successful we were at that location.
