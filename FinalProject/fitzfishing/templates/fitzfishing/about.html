About

Hi, my name is Matthew Fitzgerald. I am currently going to college at Weber State University majoring in Web Design and User Experience. I am almost done with my degree as I am on track to graduate in Spring of 2023.

As you probably already know, I am a serious fisherman. It is my favorite hobby and pastime. I have been fishing avidly for over a 10 year and over those ten years I have caught thousands of fish and have gained a lot of knowledge about fishing and how to catch fish, especially Trout.

While Trout are my area of experitse when it comes to fishing, a Trout is not actually my favorite fish. My favorite fish is a Tiger Muskie, some reasons for this are because they are a very tough fish to catch so there's always a big challenge when targeting, and that no matter how big or small they are they will always put up a good fight and they are always extremely rewarding to catch.

This website is site that I put all my fishing data that I gather from every fishing trip that I go on that is now also availible to you as well. All data on this site is from 2017 to 2021. However, there will be no data for 2017 specifically because there is not a whole years worth of data. I hope that you are able to find the information on this site useful and that it might help you during your next fishing trip.
